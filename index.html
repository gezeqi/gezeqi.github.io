About Me

Zeqi is currently a M.S. student at the Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU). He is working on the Computer Aided Geometric Design (CAGD) under the supervision of Prof. Chongyang Deng.

Zeqi received his B.Mgt. degree in Information Management and Information Systems from Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU) in 2022, where he was advised by Assoc. Prof. Xueqi Xu. During his undergraduate studies, fueled by a passion for programming, he independently mastered mutiple programming languages and explored a broad range of topics in computer science. This self-directed learning not only broadened his technical horizons but also established a robust foundation for his future academic growth.

In 2022, due to the impact of COVID-19 pandemic, Zeqi was unable to return to Hangzhou after completing his internship at SI-TECH in Chengdu. As a result, he missed the opportunity to attend his graduation photo session, which became one of his great regrets. After half a year of dedicated preparation, he successfully gained admission to graduate school and documented parts of his study journey through videos on his Bilibili channel (@gezq0601). He firmly believes that if given the chance to return to his alma mater, he will continue to pursue his academic dreams in this hallowed land of learning.
